# Agrim Education - 30-Day Launch & Marketing Checklist

## Week 1: Foundation & Visibility
- [ ] **Google Business Profile (GBP)**
  - [ ] Claim/Verify listing for "Agrim Education" in Faridabad.
  - [ ] Add all services: MBBS Abroad, MBBS India, FMGE Coaching.
  - [ ] Upload high-quality office photos and team photos.
  - [ ] Add at least 5 FAQs to the profile.
  - [ ] **Action**: Ask 5 trusted friends/past clients for the first reviews.
- [ ] **Social Media Setup**
  - [ ] Create/Optimize Instagram & Facebook pages.
  - [ ] Bio should include: "Trusted MBBS Consultants | 100% Admission Assistance | Call: +91-9999177149".
  - [ ] Post 1: "Meet Our Team" (Doctor & Counselors).
  - [ ] Post 2: "Services We Offer" carousel.
- [ ] **Analytics Check**
  - [ ] Verify Google Analytics 4 is recording traffic.
  - [ ] Verify "Book Free Counselling" form fills are tracking as specific events/conversions.

## Week 2: Content & Trust Building
- [ ] **Content Seeding**
  - [ ] Publish 2 Blog Posts:
    - "Top 5 Low-Cost Medical Universities in Russia for 2026"
    - "MBBS in India vs Abroad: A Realistic Cost Comparison"
  - [ ] Create a "Student Success" highlight on Instagram with the testimonials provided.
- [ ] **WhatsApp Marketing**
  - [ ] Set up WhatsApp Business with a catalog of services (University PDFs).
  - [ ] Set up an auto-reply: "Thanks for contacting Agrim Education. To book a free counseling call, click here: [Your Link]".

## Week 3: Lead Generation (Paid & Organic)
- [ ] **Google Ads (Search)**
  - [ ] Launch "MBBS Consultant Delhi" campaign.
  - [ ] Target keywords: "MBBS admission consultant", "Medical counsellors in Faridabad".
  - [ ] Set budget: ₹500 - ₹1000/day initial test.
- [ ] **Facebook/Instagram Lead Ads**
  - [ ] Creative: "Download Fee Structure 2026" PDF (Lead Magnet).
  - [ ] Target: Parents (Age 40-55) in Delhi NCR with interests in "NEET", "Medical School".

## Week 4: Nurture & Optimization
- [ ] **Email Automation**
  - [ ] Set up auto-responder for website form leads:
    - Email 1 (Immediate): "Thank you + Here is the University Comparison Sheet".
    - Email 2 (Day 2): "How to choose the right country? (Video/Guide)".
    - Email 3 (Day 5): "Success Story: Meet Sarthak from BSMU".
- [ ] **Review & Refine**
  - [ ] Check Search Console for impressions on "MBBS abroad consultant".
  - [ ] Call everyone who filled the form but didn't book a meeting.
  - [ ] Response time audit: Ensure all leads are called within 2 hours.

## Post-Launch SEO Routine
- [ ] **Weekly**: 1 New Blog Post targeting long-tail keywords (e.g., "Is MBBS in Uzbekistan safe for Indian girls?").
- [ ] **Monthly**: Request reviews from active students/parents.
- [ ] **Quarterly**: Update university fee structures and currency exchange rates.
